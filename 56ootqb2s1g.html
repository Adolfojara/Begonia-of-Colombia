5. Begonia diegonensis Jara sp. nov. 
 
Herb, to 1 m. Branches glabrous. Stipules early deciduous, 7.1–9.2 mm long, × 1.9–3.3 mm. Infloresence unisexual in monoecious plants.

Habitat and distribution

endemic to the North and Western part of the Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta, between 1400 and 2000 m.
