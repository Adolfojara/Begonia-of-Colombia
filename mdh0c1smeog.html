Herb, 0.15–0.4 m. Branches more or less covered with ferrugineous crispo-pilose pubescence. Stipules deciduous, 2–6 mm long, ovate, setulose or obtuse. Petiole 2–15 mm long. Foliar lamina mostly straight; 3; 8 cm; ovate, or elliptic; strongly asymmetryc; apex acuminate, or acute; margin setose-serrate, or doubly serrate; above sparcely hirtellous, or glabrous.
