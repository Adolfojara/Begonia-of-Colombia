A revision of the Begonia (Begoniaceae) section Casparya in Colombia is provided, including a generic description, illustrations, and map of distribution for the species. This section is distributed from Costa Rica to Peru, with the highest diversity in the Colombian Andes. The sections is easly recognised by the type of fruit, and form a clade. A total of 30 species are treated, five described as new species: Begonia lawrencei sp. nov., Begonia guanentana sp. nov., Begonia mamapachensis sp. nov., Begonia galeanoi sp. nov., Begonia cerro sp. nov. and Begonia diegoensis sp. nov., and two new varieties Begonia kalbreyerii var. orquidensis, and Begonia silverstonei var. brevipetiolata
