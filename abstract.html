A revision of the \textit{Begonia} (Begoniaceae) section \textit{Casparya} in Colombia is provided, including a generic description, illustrations, and map of distribution for the species. This section is distributed from Costa Rica to Peru, with the highest diversity in the Colombian Andes. The sections is easly recognised by the type of fruit, and form a clade. A total of 36 species are treated, six described as new: Begonia pomecana sp. nov., Begonia garagoana sp. nov.,Begonia guanentana sp. nov.Begonia galeanoi} sp. nov., \textit{Begonia perijaensis} sp. nov. and \textit{Begonia diegonensis} sp. nov.
