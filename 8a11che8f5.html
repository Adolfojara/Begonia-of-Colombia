}.
Seed dispersal in Casparya may be even more limited than in other sections, where the wings enhance the seed expulsion \citep{faegri1979principles}. The precise mechanism of seed dispersion have not been reveled yet in Casparya, however because the lack of wings, the position of the dehiscence, and the ornamentation in the testa the most probable mechanism is rain ballistic dispersal. Change from wind to water as dispersal mechanism have sense for a group that inhabit mostly in the understory of wet forest, with high levels of rain and low wind energy.
