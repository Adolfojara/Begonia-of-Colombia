6. Begonia diffusa L.B. Sm. & B.G. Schub. Caldasia 4(16): 32–33, t. 6. 1946. TYPE. COLOMBIA. Santander: Quebrada de Pais (Paez?), north of La Baja, ca. 3200 m, E.P. Killip & A.C. Smith 18772 (holotype, NY; isotypes, GH, US).
