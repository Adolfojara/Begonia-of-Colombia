Taxonomic treatment

Begonia aguilarii Jara & Zabala-Rivera. Novon 26 (4): 355–363. TYPE: Colombia: Santander: Mpio. El Hato, vereda Hoya Negra, predio Golcondas [Golconda], quebrada La Vega, Parque Nacional Natural Serranía de los Yariguíes y su zona de amortiguamiento, 6º36'27.8"N 73º21'33.7"W, 2000 m, 20 Feb 2010.  Aguilar-Cano 516 (hotype, FMB!; isotype, UIS!).

Distribution and habitat

This species overlap partially its distribution with the close relative B. pax, growing in a northern region of the Yariquies serrania. It has been recorded from 1400 to 2200 m.

Taxonomic notes

This is a species close related with other species from the north of the eastern cordillera. From \textit{Begonia pax} this can be distinguished because the glabrous lamina below.
