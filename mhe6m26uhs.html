Etymology

The name honors to the colombian botanist Lorenzo Uribe Uribe (1900-1980) [Lalinization of his spanish name Lorenzo as Lawrence], who contributed to the education in secoundary school with his book "Botánica para el Bachillerato", and was professor of botany in the Universidad Nacional of Colombia, was director of the journal Caldasia (1952-1958), and was particullarly interested in Begonia, being the author of Begoniaceae in the edition of the Jose Celestino Mutis iconography, and collected numerous specimens of this family, including some specimens of this species.
