16. Begonia lawrencei Jara & Zabala-Rivera. COLOMBIA: Boyacá: Municipio de Moniquirá, vereda Alto Colorado, Km 24, via de Arcabuco-Moniquirá, costado sur-occidental, 5º48'51.07 N, 73º29'52,78 W, 2375 m, J.C. Zabala-Rivera J. C., W. Bravo-P. & Z. López-R. 2696 (holotype, ANDES; isotypes, COL, UPTC). 
Caulescent herb, to 1.8 m high. Stem erect, branching; internodes to 7.4 cm long, to 9. mm thick, succulence, green to reddish brown, indumentum
